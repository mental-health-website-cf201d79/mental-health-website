Our quiz is designed to quickly screen for the unknown presence of a mental health condition. 26% of the population is estimated to have a mental health condition, yet only 20% of the population has a mental health diagnosis. This difference suggests a prevalence of undiagnosed and untreated mental health conditions. This screening quiz may open an opportunity to an improved quality of life.

Mental health conditions have real impact on people. Spreading awareness can expand our understanding of each other. More understanding can lead to re-evaluation of our individual actions and social norms. Hopefully more awareness can lead to incremental changes that can make the world can be a little more accomodating to our individual differences.
