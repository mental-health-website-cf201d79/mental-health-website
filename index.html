The Questionnaire

The quiz is designed to quickly screen for the unknown presence of certain mental health conditions. 26% of the population is estimated to have a mental health condition, yet only 20% of the population has a mental health diagnosis. That difference suggests there is common prevalence of undiagnosed mental health conditions.

Mental Health Day

This Sunday, October 10, is World Mental Health Day! Mental health conditions are common and have real impact on people. Spreading awareness will increase our understanding of one another. More awareness will lead to incremental changes that will make the world more welcoming to our individual differences.
